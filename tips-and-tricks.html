2048 Game Tips & Tricks

Playing 2048 is very easy, you can get into the habit of going through the game regardless of what you are doing. It is not a good idea. Take your time, after all, there is no penalty for thinking about your move. Also, try to predict where the new blocks might go and how you can implement them. Something similar to playing chess.

To win the game, you must have a specific strategy. If you don't have one, you will most likely not go beyond 512. Therefore, a good strategy is to always keep the highest value chip in one of the corners. Just pick a corner and direct all your tiles there. It is not as easy as it sounds, but you can quickly master it. To stop the higher value tile and leave "the king's place," try placing tiles around it. Preferably, it has to be the alternative large tiles. Master this easy method and thanks to this trick you may quickly attain 1024 or even win the sport of 2048!

Once you've placed your highest tile value in the lower-right corner, don't move it. To make sure it doesn't move, you need to make the last row always fill by pressing the down arrow so that using the Left and Right arrow does not move the higher value tile. Basically, this is the most important trick of Game 2048: keep the highest tile in the corner and do not move it.
